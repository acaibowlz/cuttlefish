## Text formatting

Body copy can be **bold**, *italic*, ~~struck through~~, or `inline code`.
Links look [like this](https://example.com), and bare URLs such as
https://example.com become links too.

## Headings and the table of contents

Every `##` and `###` heading gets an anchor, so the template can build a
table of contents from them.

### A nested heading

Third-level headings show up indented in the table of contents.
